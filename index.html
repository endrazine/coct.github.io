VALUES

Peace Love Unity Respect

RESPECT

We respect each others, all humans, all living forms, and all non living things as well. We respect our environment, our neighbours and our surroundings as we ll as the rest of mankind.

CONCIOUSNESS

We seek higher consciousness through dialogue and interaction with each others.

HAPPYNESS

We seek to spread and receive the love of all things in the Universe.

CREATIVITY

We seek to better the world through the act of creation, artistic, technical or spiritual.

INCLUSIVENESS

We seek to show open mindedness and include everyone without distinction.

CURIOSITY

Cultivate curiosity to the world, to others, experiment and discover.

Worship

We worship Women, Fertility, the Moon, the Coit Tower, Mother Nature. Worship often takes the form of tribal dancing and incantations.
